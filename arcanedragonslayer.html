    Antichroma Arcane Dragonslayer Icy Sun

Created with two other NJIT students (Ashley Yang and Chris Nicodemus), Arcane Dragonslayer is a top-down roguelite/twin stick shooter where you use magic to slay mighty dragons!
I worked on the original concept, creature concept art, enemy AI, general programming, sound design and music composition, game design, 3D models, and some of the 2D artwork.
