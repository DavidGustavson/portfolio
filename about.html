My Name is David Gustavson, and I make games.
My particular passions lie in game design, creature design, and soundtrack creation. However, I am also skilled in programming and 3D asset creation. I have participated in numerous Game Jams, leading to smaller games such as Antichroma. I have also worked on larger projects, such as Arcane DragonSlayer, and currently have a new game in the works: Icy Sun.
I am a recent graduate from New Jersey Institute of Technology, having graduated in 2023.
