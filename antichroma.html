    Antichroma Arcane Dragonslayer Icy Sun

Created with two other NJIT students for a 48-hour game jam, Antichroma is a simple platformer.
You play as Achrom, a little skull-shaped fellow that goes around striking all the whimsical life around them in an effort to drain the world of color, bringing it all to grey.
