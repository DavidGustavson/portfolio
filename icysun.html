    Antichroma Arcane Dragonslayer Icy Sun

Icy Sun is a new horror-esque action game in the works.
In a world where demons turned the sun cold, the last few survivors of humanity are confined to a volcanic isle where the last of the heat is.
You play as Alistair, a person who strangely enough is capable of using magic. New to the isle, Alistair must fend off the demon horde without assistance from the other villagers. Wield your blade and spells to slay the demons and defeat powerful bosses!
